Roman Pankov's Pasta Recipe

Pasta

Recipe by: Roman Pankov

Equipment

  • Large pot
  • Stove
  • Strainer

Directions

  1. Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add salt and pasta to the boiling water.
  3. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  4. Drain the pasta and mix with sauce.
